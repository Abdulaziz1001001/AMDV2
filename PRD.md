# Product Requirements Document (PRD)
**Project:** AMD United Contracting Company - HR & Attendance Web Platform

## 1. Product Overview
A centralized, internal web application designed to manage HR operations, attendance, and site-level interactions for office staff and construction site workers. The goal is to digitize manual processes, ensure accurate payroll data, and provide real-time visibility into workforce deployment.

## 2. User Roles
* **Admin (HR / System Admin):** Full access to reporting, system configuration, organization oversight, and final approvals.
* **Manager (Project/Site Manager):** Can view site/project allocations, approve leave/overtime for their team, and view team attendance.
* **Employee (Site Worker / Engineer):** Can track time, submit requests, view announcements, and manage their personal HR profile.

## 3. Core Features (Scope)
**Attendance & Time Tracking:**
* Check-in / Check-out functionality.
* Break logging and End-of-Day closing.
* Overtime requests and manager/admin approval workflows.
* Early checkout requests.

**Leave & HR Management:**
* Leave management system (Requests + Approval chains).
* HR Self-Service (Profile updates, document uploads/management).
* Onboarding checklist support for new hires.

**Operational & Site Management:**
* Project / Site management and workforce allocation.
* Shift management and roster scheduling.
* Safety incident reporting (critical for construction sites).
* Interactive Directory / Org Chart.

**Communication & Reporting:**
* Company-wide announcements.
* Admin notification system.
* Audit logs for tracking system changes and user actions.
* Admin reporting dashboard with PDF and XLSX export capabilities.
